Izetta: The Last Witch Review

Overview

Izetta: The Last Witch is a Japanese anime series. It takes place during WWII, in the beginning of the series Izetta attempts to travel around different countries to keep her witch heritage a secret, but with the outbreak of war Izetta devotes herself to protect princess Fine and Eylstadt, the country where war breaks out. With her anti-tank rifle as her "broom" and powerful magic Izetta defeats Germanians, or the Nazis.

Should you watch Izetta the Last Witch?

Izetta the Last Witch is an interesting anime series with a great plot, Episode 1 catches the viewers' and makes the viewer want to continue watching the series. So what can possibly go wrong with combining World War II and a girl that has powerful magic?

The Good

The good thing about this series is that it has an interesting plot from the start, although the very beggining, episode 1, might has a boring start to the series. But as it continues it gets a little more intersting and makes the audience engaged to the series.

The Bad

The bad thing about this series is that it is only 12 episodes and the reason for that is because the directors did not have any ideas left to come up with more episodes. For the same reason the series might be a little repetitive.
