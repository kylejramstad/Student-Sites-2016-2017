The Legend of Zelda is an adventure game released in 1986 which you play as Link . Link is often given the task of rescuing Princess Zelda and the kingdom of Hyrule from Ganon , who is the principal antagonist of the series . Your mission is to become the hero of Hyrule .



Tetris is a tile-matching puzzle videogame ; which your mission of the game is to manipulate these objects by moving each one sideways and rotating it by 90 degree units to create a horizontal line of ten units without gaps . Tetris is an adictive game which you'll fall in love with.
